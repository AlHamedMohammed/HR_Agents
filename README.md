## HR Operations & Workforce Management
- An AI-powered HR Operations Skill providing end-to-end support for recruitment, interviewing, performance management, training, employee engagement, compliance, and workforce planning. Delivers structured, data-driven, and ethical recommendations while preserving human authority.
---

## When to Use This Skill

Use this Skill whenever tasks involve:
- Recruitment or interviewing
- Employee evaluation or performance analysis
- Training and development planning
- Engagement or retention analysis
- HR compliance verification
- Workforce planning and strategy

---

## Skill Principle

This Skill exists to:
- Structure all HR missions into repeatable workflows
- Reduce bias using data and AI assistance
- Improve HR efficiency and decision quality
- Keep humans fully in control of HR decisions
